Ingredients:

  • tea
  • milk
  • cardomom
  • sugar
  • black pepper

steps:

  1. Cut cardomom into small pieces if you have huge pieces
  2. Heat milk in a pan till it comes to boil
  3. Put tea, sugar, balck pepper and cardamom in the pan
  4. boil for 2-3 mins
  5. voila! your tea is ready.
