Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • mayonaise
  • sandwich sauce
  • cucumbers
  • chilli
  • onions
  • tomatoes

steps:

  1. Cut chilli into small pieces
  2. Slice cucumbers, tomatoes and onions to thin slices
  3. On one side of bread, put mayonaise
  4. on the other side, put the sauce
  5. arrange onions,chilli,tomatoes and cucumbers as you wish between the bread slices
  6. grill the bread or toast it
  7. cut the bread into triangular pieces.
  8. voila! your sandwiches are ready.
